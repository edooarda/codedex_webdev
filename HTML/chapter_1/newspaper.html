WHAT THEY'RE READING

By Lawrence Malkin, International Herald Tribune

Murray L. Weidenbaum, head of the Center for the Study of American Business at Washington University, is reading "Inner Circles: How America Changed the World," a memoir by Alexander Haig.

"I know Al Haig but I had no idea what a pivotal role he had played
with MacArthur in the Far East, as commander of NATO in Europe, and
so many other places."

A version of this article appears in print on Sept. 7, 1993 in The International Herald Tribune.
